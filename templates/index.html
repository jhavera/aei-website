The AVERA Architecture: Advanced, Adaptable, and Secure.

At Avera Enterprises, our core innovation is the Advanced Virtualized Enterprise Reconfigurable Architecture (AVERA). This is a specialized machine learning technology designed to deploy high-performance AI models across diverse and resource-constrained environments.

How It Works: A Proven Workflow.

The Orchestrator (Manager)

Continuously monitors for new data files to initiate the processing workflow, acting as the central command.

The Ingestion Engine (Data Reader)

Parses specialized files like NITF, extracting and saving necessary data for analysis.

The Analysis Core (ATR)

Runs pre-trained models to detect and classify targets, generating annotated images with bounding boxes and confidence scores.

The Integration Layer (UCI Adapter & OMS CAL)

Ensures rapid, cost-effective integration into existing government systems by generating and queuing standard messages.

The AI-Powered Copilot (ARTUU)

Acts as a copilot with a conversational and command-based interface for human operators.

Comprehensive Adaptation Techniques.

Our approach is distinguished by our comprehensive methods for adapting AI models built upon foundational open models, including IBM's Granite and Meta's Llama.

  • Fine-Tuning

    We use Low-rank adaptation (LoRA) and Quantized Low-Rank Adaptation (QLoRA) to efficiently fine-tune base models on small, domain-specific datasets for superior accuracy.

  • Prompt Engineering

    We refine specific input queries to guide the model's output without altering its core architecture, which is critical for real-time applications.

  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

    We integrate external knowledge bases to ground the model in accurate, mission-specific information, reducing the risk of generating inaccurate content.
